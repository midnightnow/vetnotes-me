# Specialist Outreach Plan: Founding Fellows

## Objective
Recruit 12 board-certified specialists as “Founding Fellows” to seed credible clinical content for the AIVA Academy and establish the AIVA clinical authority model within veterinary referral networks.

## Target Cohort
- Emergency/Critical Care: VECCS or equivalent
- Internal Medicine: ACVIM/ECVIM
- Surgery: ACVS/ECVS
- Dermatology: ECVD/ACVD
- Cardiology: ACVIM (Cardiology)
- Oncology: ACVIM (Oncology)
- Neurology: ACVIM/ECVN
- Ophthalmology: ACVO/ECVO
- Anesthesia: ACVA/ECVA
- Diagnostic Imaging: ACVR/ECVDI
- Behavior Medicine: ACVB/ECBM
- Exotic/Zoo Medicine: ECZM/ACZM

## Execution Priority

### Priority 1 — Dermatology (First Specialist Vertical)
Dermatology is the first vertical to activate for three reasons:
1. FNQ / Cairns referral density is high enough to support a local 3-contact outreach loop.
2. The clinical schema is narrow enough to define, validate, and ship within one sprint.
3. The referral friction is visible and measurable: incomplete washout documentation and missing cytology details generate repeat phone calls that the specialist can quantify immediately.

Dermatology execution assets:
- `Academy/Dermatology/derm-outreach-sequence.md` — email sequence + contact table
- `Academy/Dermatology/derm-interview-card.md` — 15-minute audit script
- `Academy/Dermatology/derm-leader-format-draft.md` — 4-point protocol schema with AIVA validation rules, VetSorcery NLP extraction targets, and Doolittle lab schema

Target: 3 FNQ Cairns Derm specialists contacted by [date], 1 interview within 7 days, schema finalized after 2 of 3 interviews agree.

### Priority 2 — Emergency/Critical Care (Second Vertical)
Emergency/Critical Care is the next logical expansion once the Derm protocol is locked. The AIVA phone routing capability delivers immediate value here: after-hours emergency triage, multi-location overflow routing, and direct calendar booking into VetSorcery.

### Priority 3 — All Remaining Specialties
Academic Registrars and niche solo-specialist clinics follow once the first two verticals have published modules and have applied for AVA/RCVS accreditation.

## Outreach Tier
Priority 1: Local Cairns / North Queensland specialists already referring to the practice. Priority 2: Academic Registrars / Residents building a digital CV. Priority 3: Niche solo-specialist clinics with high-value expertise but no publishing platform.

## The Pitch
“Define your Gold Standard intake once. AIVA enforces it for every GP who refers to you. VetSorcery captures the required fields automatically. In exchange, we pay your AVA/RCVS membership fees for one year and give your clinic a lifetime VetSorcery Pro license.”

## Incentive Structure
- Content creation: 20% revenue share on CPD certificate sales generated by their module
- Pro license: Lifetime VetSorcery Pro access once the first module is published
- Professional fees: Paid professional membership fees for one year

## Deliverable
- 15-minute “Expert Protocol” audio/video walkthrough
- 5-question case quiz
- Cryptographic signature on the module certificate

## Timeline
Week 1: Send 3 Derm outreach emails. Week 2: 1 Derm interview scheduled and recorded. Week 3: Derm protocol locked, CPD module drafted. Week 4: Accreditation application submitted; Emergency outreach begins.
